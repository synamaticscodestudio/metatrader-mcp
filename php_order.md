Orders
Use the methods described in this section to get clients' orders. The following methods are available:
| Method | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| --- | --- |
| [OrderGet](php_orderget.md "OrderGet") | Get a trade order by a ticket. |
| [OrderGetTotal](php_ordergettotal.md "OrderGetTotal") | Get the number of open orders of a client by the login. |
| [OrderGetPage](php_ordergetpage.md "OrderGetPage") | Get open orders of a client by the login. |
| [HistoryGet](php_historyget.md "HistoryGet") | Get a closed order by the ticket. |
| [HistoryGetTotal](php_historygettotal.md "HistoryGetTotal") | Get the number of closed orders of a client by the login in the specified time range. |
| [HistoryGetPage](php_historygetpage.md "HistoryGetPage") | Get closed orders of a client by the login. |
