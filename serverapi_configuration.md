Configuration databases
Functions described in this section provide access to all settings of a trading platform. The allow receiving the current state of settings, changing them and handle events associated with their change. Configuration databases are divided based on their purpose:
  * [Common](serverapi_config_common.md "Common")
  * [Network](serverapi_config_network.md "Network")
  * [Plugins](serverapi_config_plugins.md "Plugins")
  * [Data Feeds](serverapi_config_datafeeds.md "Data Feeds")
  * [Time](serverapi_config_time.md "Time")
  * [Holidays](serverapi_config_holiday.md "Holidays")
  * [Firewall](serverapi_config_firewall.md "Firewall")
  * [Symbols](serverapi_config_symbol.md "Symbols")
  * [Spreads](serverapi_config_spread.md "Spreads")
  * [Groups](serverapi_config_group.md "Groups")
  * [Managers](serverapi_config_manager.md "Managers")
  * [History Synchronization](serverapi_config_historysync.md "History Synchronization")
  * [Gateways](serverapi_config_gateway.md "Gateways")
  * [Routing](serverapi_config_route.md "Routing")
  * [Reports](serverapi_config_report.md "Report Configuration")
  * [Mail Servers](serverapi_config_email.md "Mail Servers")
  * [Messengers](serverapi_config_messenger.md "Messengers")
  * [Automation](serverapi_config_automation.md "Automations")
  * [VPS](serverapi_vps.md "VPS")
  * [KYC](serverapi_kyc.md "KYC")
  * [Subscriptions](serverapi_config_subscription.md "Subscriptions")
  * [Floating Margin](serverapi_config_leverage.md "Floating Margin")
