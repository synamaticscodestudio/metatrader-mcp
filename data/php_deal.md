Deals
Use the methods described in this section to get clients' deals. The following methods are available:
| Methods | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| --- | --- |
| [DealGet](php_dealget.md "DealGet") | Get a deal by a ticket. |
| [DealGetTotal](php_dealgettotal.md "DealGetTotal") | Get the number of a client's deals by the login. |
| [DealGetPage](php_dealgetpage.md "DealGetPage") | Get the deals of a client by the login in the specified time range. |
