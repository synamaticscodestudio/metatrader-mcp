Volume Functions
The SMTMath class contains the following methods for working with the volumes:
| Method | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| --- | --- |
| [VolumeToInt](smtmath_volumetoint.md "VolumeToInt") | Converting a volume from double to int. |
| [VolumeExtToInt](smtmath_volumeexttoint.md "VolumeExtToInt") | Converting the increased accuracy volume from double to int. |
| [VolumeToDouble](smtmath_volumetodouble.md "VolumeToDouble") | Converting a volume from int to double. |
| [VolumeExtToDouble](smtmath_volumeexttodouble.md "VolumeExtToDouble") | Converting the increased accuracy volume from int to double. |
| [VolumeToSize](smtmath_volumetosize.md "VolumeToSize") | Converting the volume from lots to amount. |
| [VolumeExtToSize](smtmath_volumeexttosize.md "VolumeExtToSize") | Converting the increased accuracy volume from lots to amount. |
| [VolumeFromSize](smtmath_volumefromsize.md "VolumeFromSize") | Obtaining volume in lots from the volume specified as amount. |
| [VolumeExtFromSize](smtmath_volumeextfromsize.md "VolumeExtFromSize") | Obtaining extended accuracy volume in lots from the volume specified as amount. |
