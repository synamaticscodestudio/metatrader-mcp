Timing
There are two commands for receiving the working time settings of the trading platform:
| Method | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| --- | --- |
| [TimeGet](net_timeget.md "TimeGet") | Get the working time settings of the server. |
| [TimeServer](net_timeserver.md "TimeServer") | Get the current server time. |
