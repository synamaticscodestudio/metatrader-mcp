Interfaces of Configuration Databases
Interfaces described in this section provide access to all settings of a trading platform. The allow receiving the current state of settings, changing them and handle events associated with their change. Configuration databases are divided based on their purpose:
  * [Common](config_common.md "Common")
  * [Network](config_network.md "Network")
  * [Plugins](config_plugins.md "Plugins")
  * [Data Feeds](config_datafeeds.md "Data Feeds")
  * [Time](config_time.md "Time")
  * [Holidays](config_holiday.md "Holidays")
  * [Firewall](config_firewall.md "Firewall")
  * [Symbols](config_symbol.md "Symbols")
  * [Spreads](config_spread.md "Spreads")
  * [Groups](config_group.md "Groups")
  * [Floating Margin](config_leverage.md "Floating Margin")
  * [Managers](config_manager.md "Managers")
  * [History Synchronization](config_historysync.md "History Synchronization")
  * [Gateways](config_gateway.md "Gateways")
  * [Routing](config_route.md "Routing")
  * [Report](config_report.md "Report Configuration")
  * [Mail Servers](config_email.md "Mail Servers")
  * [Messengers](config_messenger.md "Messengers")
  * [Automation](config_automation.md "Automations")
  * [VPS](config_vps.md "VPS")
  * [KYC](config_kyc.md "KYC")
  * [Subscriptions](config_subscription.md "Subscriptions")
  * [Funds and ETF](config_funds.md "Funds and ETF")
  * [Additional Parameters](config_param.md "Additional Parameters")
