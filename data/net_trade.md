Trade
The methods described in this section allow conducting trade operations on client accounts. Currently, one method is implemented:
| Method | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| --- | --- |
| [UserDepositChange](net_userdepositchange.md "UserDepositChange") | Conducts balance operations on a client's account. |
| [TradeBalance](net_tradebalance.md "TradeBalance") | Conduct balance operations on an account with getting the balance deal ticket. |
