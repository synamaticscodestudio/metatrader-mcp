Functions of Monetary Units
The SMTMath class contains the following methods for working with money:
| Method | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| --- | --- |
| [MoneyAdd](smtmath_moneyadd.md "MoneyAdd") | Add sums of money in the form of two numbers of type double. |
| [MoneyEqual](smtmath_moneyequal.md "MoneyEqual") | Compare two quantities of money in the form of numbers of type double. |
| [MoneyDigits](smtmath_moneydigits.md "MoneyDigits") | Get the number of decimal places for the specified currency. |
