IMTConVPS::EnFlags
IMTConVPS::EnFlags lists the flags of the VPS sponsorship settings.
| ID | Value | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| --- | --- | --- |
| VPS_NONE | 0 | No flags. |
| VPS_SPONSOR_ACTIVE | 1 | VPS sponsorship enabled. |

The enumeration is used in the [IMTConVPS::Flags](imtconvps_flags.md "Flags") method.
