Positions
Use the methods described in this section to get clients' positions. The following methods are available:
| Method | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| --- | --- |
| [PositionGet](php_positionget.md "PositionGet") | Get a client's trade position by the symbol. |
| [PositionGetTotal](php_positiongettotal.md "PositionGetTotal") | Get the number of open positions of a client by the login. |
| [PositionGetPage](php_positiongetpage.md "PositionGetPage") | Get open positions of a client by the login. |
