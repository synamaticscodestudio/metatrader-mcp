Funds and ETFs
MetaTrader 5 enables the automation of fund operations. This includes investor and manager operations, investment management, access to charts and reports: For more details, please read the [MetaTrader 5 Administrator Help](https://support.metaquotes.net/en/docs/mt5/platform/administration/fund_etf).
The interfaces described in this section enable the management of fund settings:
  * [IMTConFund](imtconfund.md "IMTConFund") — methods for getting and changing fund settings.
  * [IMTConFundAccount](imtconfundaccount.md "IMTConFundAccount") — methods for getting and changing the list of fund managers.
  * [IMTConFundInvestor](imtconfundinvestor.md "IMTConFundInvestor") — methods for getting and changing the list of fund investors.
  * [IMTConFundSink](imtconfundsink.md "IMTConFundSink") — event handlers for fund configuration changes.
